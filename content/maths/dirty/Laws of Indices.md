Multiplying indices

$a^{m} \cdot a^{n} = a^{m + n}$

Dividing indices

$a^{m}  / a ^{n} = a^{m - n}$

Brackets with indices

$(a^{m})^{n} = a ^{m \cdot n}$

Power of 0

$a^{0} = 1$

Negative indices

$a^{-m} = \frac{1}{a^{m}}$

Fractional indices

$x^{\frac{a}{b}} = (\root { b } \of { x })^{a}$
