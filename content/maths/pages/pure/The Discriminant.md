$$x = \frac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}_\quad{\t{quadratic formula}}$$

$${b^2-4ac}_\quad{\t{discriminant}}$$

If Discriminant:
$>0$ $f(x)$ has two distinct real roots.
$=0$ then $f(x)$ has one repeated root.
$<0$ then $f(x)$ has no real roots.
